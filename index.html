Macchiato Biscotti

This decadent biscotti carries subtle notes of espresso and chocolate is topped with crunchy almonds. Twice cooked crunchiness filled with Italian tradition and pride this biscotti promises to hold up and not fall apart. A perfect reason to linger over coffee.

*Chocolate Chip Cookie

These classic chocolate chip cookies are soft and moist inside with a perfect crisp edge. Finally, a gluten free cookie true to its classic taste. Vegan option availible.

*Nutcase Cookie

A mouth-watering blend of pecans, peanut butter and chocolate chips. This cookie is a home run with a perfect blend of flavours. One will never be enough.
